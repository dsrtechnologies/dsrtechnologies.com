Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) Compliance

DSR Technologies is an Equal Opportunity Employer. We are committed to providing equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, marital status, veteran status, or any other legally protected status in accordance with applicable federal, state, and local laws.

Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)

DSR Technologies complies with the ADA and applicable state and local disability laws. We provide reasonable accommodations for qualified applicants and employees with disabilities. If you require accommodation during the application or interview process, please let us know so we can ensure your full participation.

At-Will Employment

Unless otherwise specified in a written agreement signed by an authorized company representative, all employment with DSR Technologies is at-will, meaning that either the company or the employee may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause or advance notice, to the extent permitted by applicable law.

Background Checks and Verification

Employment with DSR Technologies may be contingent upon satisfactory completion of a background check and verification of employment eligibility in accordance with federal law (Form I-9). We comply with the Immigration Reform and Control Act (IRCA) and E-Verify requirements where applicable.

Data Privacy

Your personal information will be collected and processed solely for employment consideration and will be handled in accordance with applicable privacy laws and our internal data protection policies.
